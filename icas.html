International Centers Administrative Services

About ICAS

International Centers Administrative Services (ICAS) started as a joint venture between the College of Liberal Arts and Sciences and International Programs and Studies (IPS), to provide grant and administrative services for the U.S Department of Education Title VI National Resource Centers housed under these units.

ICAS currently provides administrative support for many of the IPS units.
